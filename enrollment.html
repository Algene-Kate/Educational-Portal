Terms and Conditions

Enrollment Terms

Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before submitting your enrollment:

1. Payment Policy: Full payment is required to secure your place in the course. Payment plans are available upon request.

2. Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made 7 days or more before the course start date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 7 days before the start date will incur a 25% cancellation fee.

3. Attendance: Students are expected to attend all scheduled classes. For face-to-face courses, a minimum of 80% attendance is required to receive a certificate of completion.

4. Online Access: Students enrolled in online courses will receive access credentials at least 24 hours before the course start date. Access is non-transferable and for personal use only.

5. Course Materials: All course materials provided remain the intellectual property of the institution. Reproduction or distribution of these materials is strictly prohibited.

6. Code of Conduct: Students are expected to maintain a respectful and professional demeanor during all interactions. Any form of discrimination or harassment will not be tolerated.

7. Technical Requirements: For online courses, students are responsible for ensuring they have the necessary equipment and internet connectivity to participate effectively.

8. Data Privacy: Personal information collected will be used solely for administrative purposes related to your enrollment and will be handled in accordance with our privacy policy.

9. Course Changes: The institution reserves the right to make changes to the course schedule, instructors, or content as necessary. Students will be notified of any significant changes.

10. Certification: Certificates will be issued only upon successful completion of all course requirements and assessments.

For any inquiries, please contact our admissions office at admissions@itcourses.edu
